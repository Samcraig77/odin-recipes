Old fashioned

One of my personal favorites of all time, the smokey cocktail that leaps off the menu at me every time. There are numerous ways to get the same result as this recipe but this is my favorite way!

What you'll need

  • 1/2 teaspoon of simple syrup (add a lightly flavored one for a more unique taste)
  • 2 dashes of Old Fashioned bitters (sub for any of your favorite bitters)
  • 2 ounces of bourbon
  • Orange peel for garnish (this also adds a great complexity to the pallet)
  • ICE!!! Make sure you add ice, it helps the bourbon have more body!

Steps

  1. Add large ice cubes to a glass
  2. Pour the simple syrup over the ice cubes
  3. Add 2 or 3 dashes of bitters
  4. Stir a few times until mixed
  5. Add the 2 ounces of bourbon over the ice
  6. Garnish with the orange peel
  7. Sit in a chair and enjoy! Or make it first and sip while making the rest of your food!
